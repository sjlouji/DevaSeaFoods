Indian Tropical Agro Products Limited

In 1989, during Mr.SDK. Rajan’s business trip to Srilanka, he observed the GHERKIN – HYBRID CUCUMBER cultivation and its export potentials.With great eutrepreneumial spirit; he started INDIAN TROPICAL AGRO PRODUCTS LTD in Tuticorin, which is first of its kind in India. Indian Tropical Agro Products practiced contract farming in association with about 3000 farmers,by providing their seeds, Fertilizers and Technical know – how. And having our two processing unit in compliance with the International Food Standards at Tuticorin and Thiruvanamalai. In addition to Gherkin, we have also processed vegetables like White Onion, Color Bell Pepper and Baby Corn. And we have been exporting 500FCL’s to Europe, North America, Far East, Middle East, Meditteraneau Ocean Countries. On Several occasions, we were awarded by the Confederation of India Industry (CII) for the top Indian exporter. And we were also awarded Export Excellence Award for Special Economic Zone Ministry of Commerce & Industry Government of India. During the course of time, we could be able enter into different business activities, by starting the Marine Research Institute to nurture Marine Research students in the name of SUGANTHI DEVADOSAN MARINE RESEARCH INSTITUTE. We have our own coir pith processing and exports of about 400 FCL’s in the name of KING HYDROPONICS. As we have own Salt pan we have processed edible grade salt and marketed in the name of BEST FOODS. RAJA TOURS AND TRAVELS is another sister concern, with IATA approved for International and Domestic Air – Ticketing activities. In the Marine Food Industry, we have a closen association with Ms. DIAMOND SEA FOODS,which is one of the pioneers in sea food industry, started in 1976 at Tuticorin. Since its inception, DIAMOND SEA FOODS made giant leap in the exports of shrimps, Cuttle Fish, Squids and has been awarded with BRC, BAP, and ACC for its processing and export of marine products.
